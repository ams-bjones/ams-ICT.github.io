Mission

This Department aims to provide:

  • Every Student
    • The knowledge and skills needed to be a safe user of technologies
    • The knowledge and skills to be a digitally connected citizen
    • The knowledge and skills in ICT needed to enjoy success in other academic areas.
    • The knowledge and skills to obtain their best achievement in ICT and Computing.
  • Every Teacher
    • With the skills needed to engage every student by incorporating an appropriate level of ICT.

Tools

We in the department collaborate using a number of online tools

  • Trello Is where we store our departmental to do list, and collaborate, and communicate about the direction we are going in
  • GitHub Is where we store, and collaborate on Departmental documents such as this. Our Scheme of work is Open source, anyone can contribute, department members have permissions as set by the Head of Department
