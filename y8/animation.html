Aims

This is an introduction to Animation.

By the end of this unit you will learn:

  • Level 4
    • (Alg)Breaks down problems into subtasks
    • (IT)Makes judgements about digital content when deigning and repurposing content.
    • (IT)Recognises audience when creating digital content
    • (IT)Uses Criteria to evaluate the quality of solutions
  • Level 5
    • (Alg)Represents algorithms using a structured notation
    • (IT)Evaluates the appropriateness of digital content when designing and repurposing content.
    • (IT)Recognises ethical issues when creating digital content
    • (IT)Designs criteria to critically evaluate the quality of solutions
  • Level 6
    • (IT)Justifies choices about digital content when designing and repurposing content.
    • (IT)Evaluates usability of gvisual design features when designing content for a known audience
    • (IT)Designs Criteria for users to evaluate the quality of solutions, and uses feedback to suggest improvements and refinements
